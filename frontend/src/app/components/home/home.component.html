Welcome! The following games are available to play:

Yaniv

A card game where you are trying to get the lowest amount of points.
Once you have less than 7 points, you can call Yaniv.
However if another player has a lower score you recieve a penalty.

Cyvasse (Work in progress)

Based on the board game described in "Game in Thrones", a combination of chess, Stratego and Risk.
You can create a variant, which defines the board, the pieces and how they interact.
Then issue a challenge to find an opponent and play.
